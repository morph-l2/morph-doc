## Morph Faucet

You can claim testnet tokens through the [Morph Faucet](https://morph-rails-hoodi.morph.network/faucet).

### How to Use

1. Visit the [Morph Faucet](https://morph-rails-hoodi.morph.network/faucet).
2. Enter your wallet address (not your private key).
3. Click to claim testnet tokens.

The tokens will be sent to your wallet shortly.
